fix: Reconnect ACTION button signals after initialization (filter button not working)

## Problem
Filter button click was not triggering the filter task, showing "FilterLayers: Task failed" error.
The signal chain was broken: pushButton_action_filter.clicked ‚Üí launchTaskEvent ‚Üí launchingTask ‚Üí manage_task.

## Root Cause
ACTION button signals (FILTER, UNFILTER, UNDO, REDO, EXPORT) were defined in configuration_manager.py
but not being reconnected after widget initialization in manage_interactions().

The connect_widgets_signals() method calls manageSignal() which connects signals ONCE,
but these connections could be lost or not established properly during initialization.

## Solution
1. **force_reconnect_action_signals() Call**: Added explicit call to force_reconnect_action_signals() 
   in manage_interactions() after connect_widgets_signals(). This ensures ACTION button signals
   are ALWAYS connected after initialization, bypassing the signal cache.

2. **Enhanced Logging**: Added extensive logging in:
   - launchTaskEvent(): Log call, state, task_name, validation conditions
   - manage_task(): Log receipt, task_name, orchestrator choice
   - _connect_dockwidget_signals(): Confirm launchingTask connection

3. **Validation Logging**: Added detailed logging of blocking conditions in launchTaskEvent:
   - widgets_initialized flag
   - current_layer existence
   - current_layer.id in PROJECT_LAYERS
